About Mr. Adza

Hello! I'm Mr. Adza, a passionate educator specializing in English Language Teaching (ELT). My focus is on creating dynamic and effective learning experiences that empower students to achieve their language goals.

With a commitment to innovative teaching methodologies and a student-centered approach, I strive to foster an environment where learners can thrive and develop both linguistic competence and confidence.

Currently teaching at SMA Pelita Cemerlang, I enjoy exploring new educational technologies and pedagogical strategies to enhance the learning journey.
